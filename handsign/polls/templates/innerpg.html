How It Works :


Step 1 :-

In this window as you can see we provided you with the three section 1.Alphabets detection 2.Numbers detection 3.Words detection. Click on any window that you like.

Step 2 :-

After click on start button then you go the next page, where you see two window one with color image window and another one with gray filter window. color image window is for normal use and gray is for sign detection.

Step 3 :-

For sign lang detection you should get clear background especially for gray filter window, so that it will get your hand image and detect properly.

Step 4 :-

For detection place your any hand on gray filter window. Make sure that you can only show the hand upto your wrist to gray filter window, so that it can see your hand properly.

Step 5 :-

When you are showing your hand to gray filter window hold the hand in same positin for 10 to 15 sec, so that you can get proper prediction. Same procedure is applied for all the window.
